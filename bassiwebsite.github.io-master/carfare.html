Carfare

In the year 1997, Mr. Bassi started his own car rental company with the name of "CARFARE RENT A CAR LLC" with only 5 cars in a small office in Karama. But since then, he has never looked back.

Now, Carfare Group, in its totality, consists of Rent a Car, Bus Rental, Limousine Services, General Trucks Rental and Passenger Transport services. It has its head office in Dubai Investment Park with various branches all over Dubai, Sharjah and Abu Dhabi. It has a fleet of over 2000 cars.
