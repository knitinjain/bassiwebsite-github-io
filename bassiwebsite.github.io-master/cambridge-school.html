CIS/ Cambridge Intl School, Phagwara

Kulwinder Singh Bassi strongly believes that the corporate sector has certain obligations and responsibilities to fulfill towards the society. It was this belief that led to the opening of a world class school in 2009 in his hometown, Phagwara in Punjab, India. Cambridge International School, Phagwara is managed by the Bassi Education Society and it is a one of its kind in the state of Punjab. The school is accredited to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and Cambridge International Education UK. It also carries an accreditation that allows the school to use the ISA logo for three years.

It has been named the best international school in the city, is among the top 300 international schools in India and has also won a prestigious international award by the British Council. CIS offers world class education from nursery to grade 12, along with a wide range of activities that promote excellence, success and competence among students. Facilities such as an Olympic standard Swimming Pool, Tennis Courts ,Squash Courts, Gymnasium, Large playing area, Modern Science lab , Computer Lab , Aero Modelling Training and Horse Riding are unique to this school since others in the area lack these opportunities.
