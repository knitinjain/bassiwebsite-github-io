About

Overview

Mr. Kulwinder Singh Bassi is a businessman, based out of Dubai with multiple successful enterprises to his name. He established his first company which was a trucking business in 1988. 31 years later, he is now a common household name in the transportation industry, expanding into various other sectors. His total investment value other than his businesses is estimated to be 120 million USD.

Background

Mr. Bassi was born in 1955, in Punjab to an ex-army man father. He moved to Dubai in 1976 at a very young age, where he started his career working as a laborer. His growth as one struggling to make ends meet to the person he is now, with many businesses and philanthropy work to his name, is nothing short of awe-inspiring.

Philanthropy

Philanthropy

The Bassi Family continuously strives to support causes and lend their help to organisations and charitable causes that need their assistance. A philanthropist to the core, Mr. Bassi regularly donates to various NGOs, orphanages and schools for the welfare of girls and economically weaker sections of the society. A few projects that they have undertaken as active philanthropists are:

  • Gully School Project of Dr. Kiran Bedi: This is associated with the slums of New Delhi, India where a room in a slum area is made use to teach underprivileged children who do not have the adequate resources to learn. Mr. Bassi has diligently participated and helped this cause.
  • Eye camps in their school in Phagwara, India are organised every year, which conducts free eye operations for the underprivileged. This is conducted by Dr. Ramesh (M.D) of Punarjyot Eye Bank Society, Phagwara.
  • SNF Foundation, Dubai: The Bassi Family actively supports this Foundation of Mrs Safia Bari which plays a prominent role in transforming lives of disabled children and young adults in the UAE.

Awards and Recognition

Awards and Recognition

Mr. Bassi has been appreciated in the community as an exceptional visionary, leader and philanthropist. His wonderful story is seen as a beacon of hope to many and he has been awarded for his incredible work and entrepreneurial journey. In 2013, he was felicitated with the “Pride of India” award by Indian Business & Professional Council in 2013 for his contribution to Nation and Community. In 2018, he was also included in the list of Asia’s Greatest Leaders.

Furthermore, he is a member of IBPC (Indian Business & Professional Council). He is highly regarded in Punjabi Community and he has been involved in many occasions for helping the expats from the Punjabi Community. He is a Founding member of Guru Nanak Darbar Sikh Temple, Dubai.
